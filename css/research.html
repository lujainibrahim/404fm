Research

Human Decision-Making

I am leading a research project to understand the subtle cues & biases that inform human decision-making using the approval of tax expenses as the decision-making task. This project aims to create a crowd-sourced picture of human decision-making. You can participate in it and see how your biases compare to thousands of other users.

Senior Thesis published by IEEE

I completed my thesis on "Explainable Prediction of Acute Myocaridal Infarction using Machine Learning and Shapley Values" with my classmates Munib Mesinovic & Kai-Wen Yang. We co-authored and published a paper with our results in IEEE Access. It is the first paper to be published utilizing the ECG-ViEW II dataset for a machine learning application in healthcare. The implementation and code can be found here.

Multiplicity

I co-founded & built Multiplicity, a digital curation on the internet's place in our lives, on both a personal and socio-political level, as young people in different parts of the world. We critically examine the internet's influence, and explore ways to build a healthier and more equitable internet. For more on the project's inception and goals, read our origin story.
